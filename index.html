Backend Development

Backend is the part of the computer system or application that is not seen by the user. It is typically responsible for storing and manipulating data. The backend usually consists of three parts: servers, applications, and databases.

The other side of backend is frontend, which is the part that is seen by the user. The biggest difference being that the backend developers build how a website functions and frontend programmers build and design interface, controlling how the site looks to the user.
